Family Recipes app allows you to share recipes in a small and family-oriented community.

Users can upload their recipes using Post Recipe form, add basic styling and images, and then view all of the app's recipes with real time updates.

The recipes are organized according to a wide variety of categories, which'll help you find the recipe that works best for your next meal.

Main features:
  • Post a new recipe as mentioned above
  • Comment like and share any recipe with the rest of the community. Share button will send a link (through WhatsApp for example) that users will be able to open with this app
  • After you viewed some recipe page, the instructions and images will be saved to your device locally and will be deleted automatically after haven't been opened for a while and the app uses too much memory :)
  • Following the previous bullet, There is a support for offline recipes! Just make sure you opened the recipe before...
  • Change your displayed name (the name that written in the recipe publisher area) in app settings
  • Notifications:
    • New app version
    • New recipe
    • Comment on your recipes
    • Someone likes your recipes (turned off by default)
  • Supports both english and hebrew
  • Dark theme is available for your convenience

Hope you'll enjoy this app!
